Name: Philip Harmon
Bio:
Philip Harmon is a painter and sculptor based in the Hudson Valley, New York. His work explores the tension between light and form — from intimate still lifes to expansive landscapes — using a practice rooted in direct observation and decades of atelier training in Florence and New York City.

Philip studied at the New York Academy of Art and spent two years as a resident at the Florence Academy of Art, where he developed the rigorous draftsmanship that underpins everything he makes today. His paintings favor natural light, a limited palette, and the quiet authority of objects and figures at rest.

His work has been shown at galleries in New York, Vermont, and London. He teaches occasional workshops on oil painting and figure drawing, and accepts a small number of portrait commissions each year.
